wikipedia -

        action=opensearch
        &search=catan+board+game
        &limit=3
        &namespace=0
        &format=json
        &origin=*
    https://en.wikipedia.org/w/api.php? (this returns the info from the specific title)
        action=query
        &prop=extracts
        &exintro
